About Us

Who we are

Get to know about our company

Welcome to San Jose Automation, your trusted partner in delivering cutting-edge engineering, manufacturing, supply chain, automation, IoT, and robotics solutions. We improve manufacturing processes, implement ERP systems, and source parts from Asia to the USA. Our automation expertise covers oven and furnace automation, home automation with ESP32 controllers, and precise flow control with Alicats and PID controllers.
